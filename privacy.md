### Privacy Policy

#### 1. Introduction
Operator: Akira Takahashi (hereinafter referred to as "we") appropriately handles personal information of users in this application "myght" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We comply with Japan's Personal Information Protection Act, the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and consider the protection of sensitive information -- personal life records -- as our top priority.

#### 2. Information Collected and Legal Basis
We collect and use the following information based on contractual performance of service provision and user consent:
* Authentication Information: Email address, Google/Apple account linking information (UID, display name).
* User Profile: Name (nickname), age, target age.
* Life Event Records: Title, content (text), icon, images (auto-resized, max 3), satisfaction score (1-10), life highlights, pinned label information.
* Purchase & Billing Information: In-app purchase history, subscription status.
* App Settings & Usage Information: Language/theme settings, notification settings, device model, OS version, operation logs.

#### 3. Data Storage and International Transfer
The App clearly separates data locations according to plans and manages them using Google Firebase (USA) servers. Appropriate security measures (encryption, access control) are implemented.
* Free Version (Local Storage): Life event records and image data are stored only on the user's device. We do not view or acquire the content.
* Premium Version (Cloud Sync): For synchronization across multiple devices, text data is stored encrypted in "Firebase Firestore" and image data in "Firebase Cloud Storage".
* Analysis Processing (Offline): Graph and table generation processing is completed within the device, and record content is not externally transmitted for analysis purposes.

#### 4. Data Retention and Deletion Policy
* Normal Retention: Cloud data is retained for 1 year after premium plan termination for user convenience upon resumption. Data is promptly physically deleted after this period.
* Account Deletion: When users use the in-app account deletion function, cloud data (authentication, records, images) is immediately and completely deleted.

#### 5. Use of External Modules
We use the following Google Firebase services. We do not engage in third-party advertising or data sales.
* Firebase Authentication: User authentication and account linking.
* Firebase Firestore / Cloud Storage: Cloud sync and image storage (premium version only).
* Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics: Statistical analysis of usage and error detection (uses cookies and similar technologies).

#### 6. User Rights
Users can exercise the following rights at any time:
* Access & Correction Rights: Users can view, edit, and modify their data within the app at any time.
* Right to Erasure: Data can be completely erased by uninstalling the app (free version) or using the account deletion function (premium version).
* No Sale: We do not sell or provide users' personal information to third parties.

#### 7. Security Management and Other Matters
* Protection of Minors: This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Parents should exercise caution when children use this app.
* Data Breach: In the event of a data breach, we will promptly notify relevant authorities and users in accordance with applicable laws.
* Operator Information: Details such as address and telephone number will be provided without delay upon request based on legal requirements.

#### 8. Changes to This Policy
When changing this policy, we will notify users through in-app notifications. For important changes, we may request user re-consent.
